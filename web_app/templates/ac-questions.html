About

AgingCare (agingcare.com) is a website that allows caregivers to share their experiences as well as ask questions and participate in discussions. Caregivers have access to a variety of resources on the site such as elder law attorneys, care options, and senior housing options.

We have scraped these posts from the website with posts dating back to January of 2008, the start of the message board. You can download the data set here.

We performed natural language processing (NLP) on the data we gathered, including topic modeling and sentiment analysis. Further documentation can be found on the "Documentation" page.
