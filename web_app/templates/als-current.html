About

ALS Forums is an online forum where caregivers of patients with Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) as well as patients post to give information, ask questions, and seek support. This page contains an NLP analysis of the Current Caregivers Forum, used by caregivers who are currently caring for a patient with ALS.
*Put link to download data set*

We performed natural language processing (NLP) on the data we gathered, including topic modeling and sentiment analysis. This analysis was primarily conducted on posts containing the keywords "tele", "video", "virtual", "zoom", "skype", "phone". We separated the data into "Pre-Covid" (March 1, 2018 - February 29, 2020) and "Covid" (March 1, 2020 onwards). Further documentation can be found on the Documentation page.

Topic Modeling:

The results below show a PCA plot with each topic represented as a bubble. The larger the bubble, the higher the percentage of posts that correspond to that topic. Topics that are closer to each other on the PCA plot are more similar and topics further away are more different. The blue bars on the right show the overall frequency of a word in the data set. If you click on a topic bubble, the red bars will show the estimated number of times a word was generated by that topic. More information can be found here.
